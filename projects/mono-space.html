>>> An Unknown Dimension

It was a normal and sunny summer day in the town of Aquarel... until it wasn't anymore.
An extra-dimensional being by the name of Vexer sends Earth into chaos, creating portals to other dimensions in the process. Mono Blackray happens to be at the right place at the wrong time, and ends up in the mono-chromatic geometrical dimension of Corollorum.

>>> The Heart of Corollorum

Mono stumbles upon a laboratory marked "Lab #11720", there lies the Heart of Corollorum, a clover-shaped entity capable of modifying Corollorum's attributes. Mono accidentally absorbs it, and gains the ability to switch the dimension's colors at his will. He doesn't know it yet, but this chain of events would lead up to him going head-to-head with the being that brought him there.

>>> Mysterious Environments and Entities

Vexer's invasion wasn't limited to just Earth, and it certainly wasn't the first time it happened!
Meet new friends, discover new enemies, and find out what makes the dimension tick; you may even see things that no mere human was ever supposed to see or experience situations that humans weren't built to handle.
Corollorum is a one-way dimension, but you may find a way to escape it.

>>> Journey against an mpire

You need the Heart of Corollorum's energy to even have a shot at escaping, mastering its abilities takes time and effort. Vexer knows that you've absorbed the Heart, and will try to rip you to shreds

The Heart of Corollorum

Deep within the mysterious dimension of Corollorum lies a powerful device, known as the
Heart of Corollorum. Those who possess the heart are granted immense power, allowing them to manipulate the very fabric of the dimension and unlock new realms of possibilities. However, should the Heart be destroyed, Corollorum itself will be plunged into darkness and cease to exist.

Gameplay
