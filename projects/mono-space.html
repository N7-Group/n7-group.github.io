Welcome to Corollorum

It's a beautiful summer day in the town of Aquarel, where everything was bright and colorful - until it wasn't.
Welcome to Mono-Space, a 2D Puzzle Platformer where you take on the role of
Mono Blackray, a teenager who finds themselves trapped in an alternative dimension known as Corollorum, where everything is monochrome and geometrical.

The Heart of Corollorum

Deep within the mysterious dimension of Corollorum lies a powerful device, known as the
Heart of Corollorum. Those who possess the heart are granted immense power, allowing them to manipulate the very fabric of the dimension and unlock new realms of possibilities. However, should the Heart be destroyed, Corollorum itself will be plunged into darkness and cease to exist.
