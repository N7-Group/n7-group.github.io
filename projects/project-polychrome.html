One Space, Multiple Dimensions

It's a beautiful summer day, everything's bright and colorful, until it was not anymore.
Project PolyChrome is a 2D puzzle platformer, you play as Mono Blackray, a teen who ended up in an alien dimension called Corollorum, where everything is mono-chrome, and geometrical

The Heart of Corollorum

A device with immense power, the Heart of Corollorum grants anyone who absorbs it the power to control the dimension's colors as they please, unlocking new layers of possibilities.
If the Heart is destroyed, Corollorum will be destroyed too.
